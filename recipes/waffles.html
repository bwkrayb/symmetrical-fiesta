Waffles

Waffles are like pancakes but made in a waffle iron. You can put fruit and syrup on them and they are delicious!

Ingredients:

  • Waffle Mix
  • An egg?
    • In This
    • Trying
    • Time
  • Water
  • Pumpkin butter
  • Love
  • Waffle Iron
  • Syrup
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries

Steps:

  1. Mix waffle mix with egg and water in a bowl
  2. Heat pan on stove with heat
  3. Plug in waffle iron
  4. Cook waffles for 12 minutes or until done
  5. Warm the syrup
  6. Pour syrup all over the waffles
