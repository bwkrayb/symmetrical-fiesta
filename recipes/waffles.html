Waffles

Ingredients:

  • Waffle Mix
  • An egg?

  • Water
  • Pumpkin butter

  • Waffle Iron
  • Syrup

Steps:

  1. Mix waffle mix with egg and water in a bowl
  2. Heat pan on stove with heat
  3. Plug in waffle iron
  4. Cook waffles for 12 minutes or until done
  5. Warm the syrup
  6. Pour syrup all over the waffles
