Lasagna

These are the ingredients that you will need to make lasagna. Lasagna only reqires like 5 ingredients probably. It is very easy and can be good for lots of meal prep because it makes a lot of servings. Good luck eating all of the lasagna you are going to make!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noods (flat with squiggly sides)
  • Pasta sause (you can make your own with the sub ingredients I guess, or just buy it.)
    • Tomatoes
    • Italian seasoning
    • Roast the tomatoes or something?
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Optional:
    • Meat (turkey or beef)
  • Love

Steps:

  1. Cook the Pasta noods in water
  2. Line lasagna pan with the cooked noodles
  3. Cook meat if applicable
  4. Put cheese, meat, and sauces layered over the noodles and then make more layers of noodles and sauces and meat
  5. Bake for like 45 minutes at 400 Degrees Celsius
